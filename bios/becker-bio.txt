Gregory Becker is a computer scientist at Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory. His focus is on bridging the gap between research and production software at LLNL. His work in software productization has led him to work on Spack, a package manager for high performance computing, as well scalable I/O tools. Gregory has been at LLNL since 2015. He received his B.A. in Computer Science and Mathematics from Williams College in 2015.
